# Positioning & White-Space — the pitch, in the repo's canvas shape

**Purpose:** turn the landscape into a sharp, defensible position. Follows the `skills/positioning/SKILL.md` messaging-canvas shape (audience · promise · proof · wedge · category · elevator · hierarchy). All comparative claims here are internal analysis (allowed in pipeline mode), cited to the player docs.

---

## Audience (who, one segment)

**Primary:** privacy-conscious power individuals — solo founders, independent professionals, and "digital sovereigns" who already run their lives across many apps and would hire a team if they could afford one. They want delegation **without** handing their whole digital life to a black box they can't audit.
**Secondary:** small teams who outgrow point AI tools but can't staff a back office.

## Promise (one line)

Hand your work to a company of AI employees you actually own and can trust — every task done by a specialist, checked by a verifier, secured per-employee, with receipts you can replay.

## Proof (cited)

- **No rival puts the human atop a recursive org.** Every player surveyed is **flat or 2-tier**: Warmwind (peer Cloud Employees under one admin — `10-warmwind.md`), Flowith (Oracle→Neo — `11-flowith.md`), Aluminium/Brain (single assistant — `12`, `13`), enterprise workforce + startups (departmental, not a company — `21`, `22`), and even the "AI software company" frameworks use **fixed shallow roles** (`20-multi-agent-frameworks.md`).
- **No rival ships a dedicated verifier as a named employee.** Closest is Genspark's mixture-of-agents cross-check (`15`) and Harvey's citation-traceability (`31`) — features, not a standing verifier with veto. Our repo seats CAO/CEVO/CRT as blocking vetoes (`AGENTS.md:13,32`).
- **No rival ships per-employee least-privilege for a *personal* user.** Enterprise governance specs exist (CSA ATF, MS Agent Governance Toolkit) but are not consumer products (`41-security-and-quantum.md`).
- **Zero rivals in our category mention post-quantum crypto.** Only Category-1 device OSes inherit it (Aluminium via Android 17); our category is silent (`41`).
- **The trust-OS slot is literally vacant:** /dev/agents — the closest philosophical competitor on trust — shipped "Dreamer" then was **acqui-hired into Meta (2026-03)** (`14-dev-agents.md`). The independent, user-owned position is open.

## Wedge (why us, one sentence)

We are the only personal AI **company** — a recursive org the user chairs — fused with a **trust layer** (per-employee security, a verifier with veto, replayable receipts, post-quantum crypto) that no rival staffs as first-class roles.

*Wedge strength (self-scored vs. `positioning` rubric): 5 — it's a segment flip (personal, not enterprise) + a capability flip (recursive org + trust officers) + a model flip (user-owned/sovereign, not platform-owned). Incumbents can't copy the org *shape* + trust posture in 12 months without abandoning their enterprise model.*

## Category

**Chosen frame:** "**personal AI company**" (a company you chair) — within the broader "AI OS" category, but reframing the noun from *assistant/agent* to *company*.
**Alternatives considered:** "AI operating system" (crowded, ambiguous — three meanings, doc 40), "AI workforce" (owned by enterprise vendors, doc 21), "personal AI" (assistant connotation, too small). The "company you chair" framing is adjacent enough to map in one sentence (Category fit ~4) while carving an unowned position.

## Elevator pitch (≤30 words)

*The first personal AI company you chair: a recursive team of specialist employees — each task done by an expert, checked by a verifier, secured and quantum-safe, with receipts.*

(28 words.)

## Messaging hierarchy

- **H1:** *Chair your own AI company.*
- **H2:** *Specialist employees do the work. A verifier checks it. You only step in when it matters.*
- **3 value props:**
  1. **A company, not a chatbot** — a recursive org (executives → teams → one-task employees) you sit atop as Chairman.
  2. **Trust by construction** — per-employee security, a verifier with veto power, replayable receipts, post-quantum crypto. (No rival staffs these as roles.)
  3. **Yours, not the platform's** — runs on your existing hardware, data sovereign, never sold to Big Tech (unlike the trust OS that just went to Meta).
